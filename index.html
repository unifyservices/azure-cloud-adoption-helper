💡 CAF IP Planning Best Practices
🔹 Hub VNet: Allocate at least /16 (65,536 IPs) to accommodate future growth.
🔹 Spoke VNets: Use /20–/22 for typical workloads. Avoid over-allocating.
🔹 GatewaySubnet: Minimum /27 for VPN. Use /26 or /25 for ExpressRoute with redundancy.
🔹 AzureFirewallSubnet: Must be exactly named "AzureFirewallSubnet", minimum /26.
🔹 AzureBastionSubnet: Must be exactly "AzureBastionSubnet", minimum /26.
🔹 Reserved IPs: Azure reserves 5 IPs per subnet (first 4 + last). A /24 gives 251 usable IPs.
🔹 Avoid: 172.16.0.0/12 and 192.168.0.0/16 if using ExpressRoute to avoid BGP conflicts.
🔹 Non-overlapping: Ensure no address overlap between on-premises, VNets, and peered networks.

🔹 Platform MG: Contains Connectivity, Identity, Management subscriptions
🔹 Landing Zones MG: Corp (private) and Online (internet-facing) workloads
🔹 Sandbox MG: Developer experimentation, relaxed policies
